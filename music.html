Haunt

Summer 2016

  • Arrangement includes Acoustic, Bass, Drums, Vox, Synth, and a field recording
  • The intention for this track was to make it sound pure, so the recordings are not doctored much
  • Because of this, the mix did not feel very full, so we ended up recording sounds from outside and placed them at the beginning and end of the track
  • The airy synth towards the end of the track is a Casio CZ-3000 doused in reverb

ICU

Summer 2017

  • Arrangement includes Acoustic, Electric, Bass, Drums, and Vox
  • The reverberated guitar builds a padding at the back of the mix, while the other guitar parts add interesting twinkles in the separate channels
  • The bassline is one of the driving elements of the track, so it sits higher up in the mix
  • The vocals are double tracked and hard panned, since I did not think the vocals were powerful enough to stand on their own
